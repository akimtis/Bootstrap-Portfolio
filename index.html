The Story

Connect

Anna has been a teacher for 7 years. She teaches science and makerspace to k-12 students. She has a B.S. in Environmental Science, and an MFA in Creative Writing and Environment. She is currently working toward a certificate in full stack web development through Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. She is also working on her first children's book about the biology of the Chihuahuan Desert.

Anna is a life-long learner who wishes to inspire her students to love learning. She works to find innovative ways to prepare them for the future through desgin thinking and project-based learning. She teaches 4th grade and makerspace at a k-8 independent school in Morristown, NJ.
