About

Anna has been a teacher for 7 years. She has a B.S. in Environmental Science, and an M.F.A in Creative Writing and Environment. She is currently working toward a certificate in full stack web development through Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. Her first children's book about the biology of the Chihuahuan Desert will be published in the spring of 2017.

Anna is a life-long learner who wishes to inspire her students to love learning. She creates innovative curriculum that prepares her students for the future by encouraging practice in design thinking and other 21st century skills. She teaches science, technology, and makerspace.
