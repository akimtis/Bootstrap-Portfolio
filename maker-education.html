Growing a Maker Mindset

In the makerspace students move through process of building and testing solutions to problems called design thinking. This process helps students develop a maker mindset as they overcome setbacks by asking questions about thier process, and trying new strategies and solutions. These activites are planned to help students become flexible, creative, and perservering problem solvers.
