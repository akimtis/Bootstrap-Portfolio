I Build Apps and Websites

Data Preserves

This app helps ecology students graph and compare data sets. They will use this graph generator to find and explain trends in the data to non-scientists in the Desert Data Jam competition. This app allows quick access to graphing tools and doesn't require understanding of Excell. Developed in partnership with other full stack web developers at the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.

Glossary

There is a glossary of terms that correspond to the data. Students can use it to better understand what the data represents, and the units of the measurements.

Write Captions and Download PDFs

Students can write captions for the graphs they generate, and convert those graphs into downloadable PDFs.

Graph Zooming

Students can select parts of the graph to zoom in on specific year ranges for the data.
